CENTENNIAL ROAD CORDOVA

Centennial Road in Cordova, Cebu is a four-lane(including the bike lane so it makes four-lane) road that links the highway in Barangay Alegria to the Roro port of Cordova.
It is also known as Roro Road to the locals. The road is designed with designated bike lanes, providing a safe space for cyclists.

Centennial Road serves as an important transportation route, connecting the highway to the Roro port.
The Roro port in Cordova offers trips to various destinations, making it a significant hub for transportation and commerce.

The DoT and DPWH install a waiting shed is to provide a sheltered area for people to wait for transportation or to take a break from inclement weather condition such as rain, wind, or sun.
They typically have a roof to protect people from the elements and may also have seating and other amenities to make the waiting experience more comfortable.
